How to Rewire Your Brain for Mobile First UX

  • We need to design for the smallest devices first which will give a better overall user experience.
  • Mobile devices are not going away.

How to improve UX by Understanding Users

  • "It is really hard to design products by focus groups. A lot of times, people don't know what they want until you show it to them." -Steve Jobs
  • Be There
  • Be Useful
  • Be Quick
  • Reachability - Think about how people handle their device and where convenience.
  • Speed matters

Better Mobile UX with Strategic Menu

  • We need to care and be empathetic to our users needs.
  • Move the main navigation bar to the bottom of the page so people can easily reach.
  • Good reachability makes people think the apps run faster even though they may not.
  • Menu with a border is more effective than using a hamburger button.

Designing Intuitive and Mobile Friendly Forms

  • Make it simple.
  • When using forms only include the information that is necessary.
  • Give plenty of space for fingers to click.
  • When you can't simplify the form break it into sections.
  • Enable autocomplete on your forms
  • With passwords, give the user the option to show the password.
  • Tap a phone number to call

Designing a better Mobile Advertising User Experience

  • RESEARCH when advertising!
  • DON'T USE POP UPS
  • Make ads small and simple.
  • Users don't come to your website to look at ads.
